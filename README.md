# Superstore Excel Sales Analysis

### 📊 Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data Analysis, Power Query, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts
* **Data Presentation:** Interactive Executive Dashboard

---

## 🛠️ Project Overview & Business Problem
A retail company possesses large volumes of sales data but lacks the centralized visibility required to make sharp strategic decisions. This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key performance trends, customer behaviors, and clear optimization opportunities.

### Key Business Questions Answered:
1. What are our total sales and net profit margins?
2. Which product categories and sub-categories drive profitability?
3. Which geographic regions perform best?
4. How do aggressive discount structures impact our bottom line?

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation & Cleaning
Data cleaning and transformation were thoroughly executed using **Power Query** to ensure data integrity:
* Checked and corrected mismatched data types.
* Removed duplicate records.
* Formatted messy date fields.
* Extracted **Year**, **Month**, and **Day** attributes from order dates to create granular time-series cohorts.

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis & Key Findings

### 🛍️ Category Performance
* **Technology** is our primary growth engine, leading performance with **$836K** in sales and a robust **17%** profit margin due to strong organic customer demand.
* **Furniture** generated highly competitive sales (**$742K**) but yielded an incredibly low **2%** profit margin, signaling a critical need to adjust pricing models.

### 🗺️ Regional & Trend Analysis
* **West Region** achieved the highest overall sales performance across all geographic territories.
* **Seasonality:** Sales show significant acceleration over time, peaking sharply during the Q4 holiday stretch (**October to December**). 

### 🛑 Discount Impact Analysis
* High promotional discounting directly erodes bottom-line returns. 
* **Tables** received a massive **26% average discount**, which entirely wiped out returns and resulted in a negative profit margin of **-9%**.

---

## 💼 Business Impact & Recommendations
1. **Optimize Discount Guardrails:** Review and restrict discount policies for consistently negative-margin products like Tables and Bookcases to instantly reclaim leaking profit.
2. **Reallocate Marketing Budgets:** Focus promotional efforts heavily on high-margin categories (Technology and Office Supplies) to maximize the return on customer acquisition costs.
3. **Inventory Alignment:** Prepare supply chains, logistics, and localized marketing campaigns months ahead of the peak Q4 demand window to avoid stockouts.
