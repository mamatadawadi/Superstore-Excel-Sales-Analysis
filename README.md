# Superstore Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify sales trends, profitability performance, product performance, and opportunities for business improvement.

Using **Microsoft Excel, Power Query, Pivot Tables, and Pivot Charts**, I cleaned and transformed raw data, created an interactive dashboard, and developed business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand what drives sales and profitability across different categories, products, regions, and customer segments. The goal is to analyze performance trends and identify areas where the business can improve revenue and profit.

---

## Business Questions

- What are total sales, profit, and orders?
- Which categories generate the highest sales?
- Which sub-categories are most profitable?
- Which products contribute the most revenue?
- Which regions perform best?
- How do sales change over time?
- How do discounts impact profitability?

---

## Data Preparation

Data cleaning and transformation were completed using **Power Query**.

Key steps:
- Removed duplicate records
- Corrected data types
- Formatted date fields
- Extracted year, month, and day from order dates
- Prepared data for analysis and visualization

---

## Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel** – Dashboard creation, analysis, and visualization
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Business analysis and reporting

---

## Key Performance Indicators

- **Total Sales:** $2,297,200.86  
- **Total Profit:** $286,397.02  
- **Total Orders:** 5,009  
- **Average Discount:** 16%  
- **Overall Profit Margin:** 12%

---

## Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales ($836K) with a strong 17% profit margin, showing strong revenue and profitability performance.
- The West Region achieved the highest sales compared with other regions.
- Sales increased throughout the year, with peak performance occurring from October to December.
- Tables received a 26% average discount and resulted in a negative profit margin of -9%, indicating potential profitability issues related to pricing or discount strategies.
- Standard shipping was the most frequently used shipping method.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing categories and products to increase revenue.
- Review pricing and discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Improve inventory planning during peak sales periods.
- Invest in strong-performing regions and growth opportunities.
- Use data-driven insights to support business decisions.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the complete analytics process:

**Data Cleaning → Data Transformation → Data Analysis → Dashboard Development → Business Insights**

The final dashboard helps transform raw retail data into meaningful insights that support better business decisions.
