
•	# Superstore Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard
•	
•	## Project Overview
•	
•	This project analyzes retail sales data to understand sales performance, profitability, product performance, regional trends, and discount impact. Using Excel, Power Query, and Pivot Tables, I cleaned and transformed raw data to create an interactive dashboard and generate business insights.
•	
•	## Business Problem
•	
•	The company needs better visibility into sales drivers, profitability challenges, and areas for growth. This analysis helps identify which categories, products, and regions contribute most to business performance.
•	
•	## Tools Used
•	
•	- Microsoft Excel
•	- Power Query
•	- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
•	- Data Visualization
•	
•	## Data Preparation
•	
•	Data preparation was completed using Power Query, including removing duplicates, correcting data types, formatting dates, extracting year/month/day fields, and preparing clean data for analysis.
•	
•	## Key Metrics
•	
•	- Total Sales: $2,297,200.86
•	- Total Profit: $286,397.02
•	- Total Orders: 5,009
•	- Average Discount: 16%
•	- Profit Margin: 12%
•	
•	## Key Insights
•	
•	- Technology generated the highest sales ($836K) with a strong 17% profit margin.
•	- The West Region achieved the highest sales performance.
•	- Sales increased throughout the year, with peak performance from October to December.
•	- Tables received a 26% average discount and resulted in a negative profit margin of -9%, indicating potential profitability issues.
•	- Standard shipping was the most frequently used shipping method.
•	
•	## Business Recommendations
•	
•	- Focus on high-performing categories and products to increase revenue.
•	- Review pricing and discount strategies to improve profitability.
•	- Improve inventory planning during peak sales periods.
•	- Invest in strong-performing regions and growth opportunities.
•	
•	## Conclusion
•	
•	This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow from data cleaning and transformation to dashboard creation and business insights. The analysis helps convert raw retail data into actionable information for better decision-making.
