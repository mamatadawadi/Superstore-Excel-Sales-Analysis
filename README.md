# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end Excel analytics project that transforms raw retail sales data into an interactive dashboard and actionable business insights using **Microsoft Excel**, **Power Query**, **Pivot Tables**, and **Pivot Charts**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore retail sales data to evaluate sales performance, profitability, regional trends, product performance, and the impact of discounts. The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow—from data preparation and transformation to dashboard development and business reporting—supporting data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data but often lack clear visibility into sales performance and profitability. This project helps identify key revenue drivers, profitable product categories, regional performance, seasonal trends, and pricing opportunities through an interactive Excel dashboard.

## 🔄 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** before analysis.

Data preparation included:

- Removing duplicate records
- Correcting data types
- Formatting date fields
- Extracting Year, Month, and Day from Order Date
- Preparing a clean dataset for Pivot Tables and dashboard reporting

## 💡 Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales (**$836K**) while maintaining a **17% profit margin**.
- The West region achieved the highest sales performance.
- Sales increased throughout the year, with peak performance from **October to December**.
- Standard shipping was the most frequently used shipping method.
- Tables received an average discount of **26%** and recorded a **-9% profit margin**, indicating that discount strategies may be reducing profitability.

---

## 📋 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing products and categories.
- Review pricing and discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Improve inventory planning before peak sales periods.
- Invest in high-performing regions to support business growth.
- Monitor profitability alongside sales to support better business decisions.
