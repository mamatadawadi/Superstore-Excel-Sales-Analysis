•	# Superstore-Excel-Sales-Analysis
•	### Excel | Power Query | Pivot Tables | Data Visualization | Business Insights
•	
•	## Project Overview
•	
•	This project analyzes retail sales data to identify sales performance, profitability trends, customer behavior, and areas for business improvement. 
•	
•	Using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and Pivot Tables, I cleaned, transformed, and analyzed sales data to create an interactive dashboard that helps management understand revenue drivers, profitability challenges, and growth opportunities.
•	
•	---
•	
•	# Business Problem
•	
•	A retail company has large amounts of sales data but needs better visibility into:
•	
•	- Which categories generate the most revenue
•	- Which products drive profitability
•	- Which regions perform best
•	- How sales change over time
•	- How discount strategies impact profit margins
•	
•	The goal of this project is to provide actionable insights that support data-driven business decisions.
•	
•	---
•	
•	# Business Questions
•	
•	The analysis answers the following questions:
•	
•	1. What are the total sales and total profit?
•	2. How many orders were placed?
•	3. Which product categories generate the highest sales?
•	4. Which sub-categories generate the highest profit?
•	5. Which products contribute the most revenue?
•	6. Which regions have the strongest sales performance?
•	7. How do sales change throughout the year?
•	8. Which sub-categories have profitability issues?
•	9. How do discounts impact profit margins?
•	
•	---
•	
•	# Data Preparation & Cleaning
•	
•	Data preparation was completed using **Power Query**.
•	
•	Steps performed:
•	
•	- Removed duplicate records
•	- Checked and corrected data types
•	- Formatted date fields
•	- Extracted Year, Month, and Day from order dates
•	- Created calculated columns for analysis
•	- Prepared clean data for Pivot Tables and dashboard development

•	# Dashboard Analysis
•	
•	## Sales Performance by Category
•	
•	| Category | Sales |
•	|---|---:|
•	| Technology | $836,154 |
•	| Furniture | $741,999 |
•	| Office Supplies | $719,047 |
•	
•	**Insight:**
•	Technology generated the highest sales among all categories, showing strong customer demand.
•	
•	---
•	
•	## Profitability Analysis
•	
•	Key findings:
•	
•	- Technology generated $836K in sales with a strong 17% profit margin.
•	- Furniture generated similar sales ($742K) but had lower profitability.
•	- Some sub-categories such as Tables and Bookcases showed negative profit margins.
•	
•	**Business Impact:**
•	Review pricing, costs, and discount strategies for low-profit products.
•	
•	---
•	
•	## Regional Sales Analysis
•	
•	| Region | Sales |
•	|---|---:|
•	| West | $725,457 |
•	| East | $678,781 |
•	| Central | $501,239 |
•	| South | $391,721 |
•	
•	**Insight:**
•	The West region achieved the highest sales performance.
•	
•	**Business Impact:**
•	Focus growth strategies and resources on strong-performing regions.
•	
•	---
•	
•	## Monthly Sales Trend
•	
•	Highest sales months:
•	
•	- November: $271,693
•	- September: $248,989
•	- December: $248,765
•	
•	**Insight:**
•	Sales increased during the final months of the year, showing seasonal demand.
•	
•	**Business Impact:**
•	Improve inventory planning and seasonal marketing strategies.
•	
•	---
•	
•	## Discount Impact Analysis
•	
•	| Sub-Category | Avg Discount | Profit Margin |
•	|---|---:|---:|
•	| Tables | 26% | -9% |
•	| Bookcases | 21% | -3% |
•	
•	**Insight:**
•	Tables received a 26% average discount and resulted in a negative profit margin of -9%, indicating that discount strategies may be affecting profitability.
•	
•	**Business Impact:**
•	Review discount policies to protect profit margins.
•

•	---
•	
•	# Recommendations
•	
•	Based on the analysis:
•	
•	1. Review discount strategies for products with negative profit margins.
•	2. Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories such as Technology.
•	3. Improve profitability by analyzing product costs and pricing strategies.
•	4. Prepare inventory and promotions ahead of peak sales months.
•	5. Expand successful strategies from high-performing regions.
